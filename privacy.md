# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** March 27, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Justin Stewart ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects information in connection with our SMS and voice communication services.

## Information We Collect

- **Phone numbers** you provide when interacting with our services
- **Call recordings and transcripts** from voice calls
- **Appointment details** such as dates, times, and service information
- **SMS message content** sent to and from our platform

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

- Send follow-up SMS messages related to your inquiry or service
- Send appointment confirmations and reminders via SMS
- Generate call summaries for service quality and record-keeping
- Respond to your requests and provide customer support

## Consent

By providing your phone number and engaging with our services, you consent to receive SMS messages and phone calls at the number provided. Message and data rates may apply. Message frequency varies.

## Opting Out

You may opt out of receiving SMS messages at any time by replying **STOP** to any message. You will receive a one-time confirmation message, and no further messages will be sent unless you re-subscribe. You may also reply **HELP** for assistance.

## Data Sharing

We do **not** sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. We may share information only when required by law or as necessary to provide our services (e.g., with our telephony service providers under strict data protection agreements).

## Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy or as required by law. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

## Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure.
